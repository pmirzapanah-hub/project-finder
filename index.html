Features

Everything your office needs.
Nothing it doesn't.

Built specifically for structural and civil engineering firms who work with hundreds of project folders every day.

⚡

Instant Folder Search

Type a project number and jump to the folder in under a second. Searches your network drive recursively, no matter how deep the folder structure.

👥

Client CRM

Store client contacts, phone numbers, and notes directly linked to each project. No separate CRM software needed — everything in one place.

📧

Email Chain Tracking

See every email sent and received for a project in one view. Connect your Microsoft 365 or Outlook account and the chain builds automatically.

📝

Email Templates

Pre-built templates for RFIs, submittals, proposals, and status updates. Customise them once, use them forever — with project details auto-filled.

🔗

Microsoft 365 Sync

Send emails directly through your Outlook account. Replies land in your Project Finder chain — no switching between apps.

🖥️

Desktop Toolbar

Lives quietly on your Windows desktop. One click to search, open folders, call clients, or check emails. Zero clutter, maximum speed.
